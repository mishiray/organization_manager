About BritInvest

Britinvest is a land banking scheme by Brit Property Nigeria Limited. In the concept of banking, we are not just having your money in our custody but also giving you the opportunity to grow your money with great value.

Brit invest offers investors the opportunity to invest in our fast-appreciating estates and earn returns in a short period of time. Our return-on-investment rates are very competitive and safe as we march each investment subscribed for to the size(s) of a plot land(s). Our estates on the other hand, are fast appreciating.

How it Works

Brit invest introduces a better approach to investment as this product offers different packages to be taken advantage of. The product not only offers 15% and 33% returns in 6 months and 1 year respectively but also presents you the opportunity to be a land owner.

Brit Invest operates on some of our most valuable estates, upon subscription to any of the estates of your choice, investors are offered a plot or plots of land as applicable – as a collateral on investment. The product has been designed to enable investors with varying budgets benefit optimally. With multiple options to choose from, the return on investment is guaranteed at the end of each cycle. .

Invest Now (15%)

Each investor has three options to explore at the end of the 6 and 12-months cycles respectively:

  • Cash out
  • Roll over the investment (reinvest)
  • Take possession of the land or lands as the case may be.

Where the investor opts to cash out, a postdated-cheque containing the total expected amount (capital and interest) will be issued to the investor at the subscription stage.
