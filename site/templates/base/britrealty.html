About BritRealty

Did you just move into Lagos?
Are you based in Lagos but need to change location?
Do you need an office space for your company or business?

Brit-Realty is the name to call on for all your relocation needs.

This is the arm of Brit Property Nigeria Ltd that caters to the ever present and ever increasing accommodation challenge prevalent in the Lagos Metropolis. We provide agency services to clients who may desire to buy or rent properties in Lagos.

Our scope is the whole of Lagos Metropolis but with particular emphasis on choice areas and properties that offer modern and state of the art features and facilities to suit the preferences and needs of our clients, both individual and corporate. .

We further protect the interest of our clients by ensuring that the properties we provide are non-contentious and free from all forms of restrictions and encumbrances capable of interfering with peaceful possession.
