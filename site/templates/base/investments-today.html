About BritInvest

Britinvest is a land banking scheme by Brit Property eria Limited, a fast growing real estate company in Nigeria.

Britinvest offers investors the opportunity to invest in our fast appreciating estates and earn returns in a short period of time.

How it Works

Britnvest introduces a better approach to investment as this product offers different packages to be taken advantage of. The product not only offers 15% and 33% returns in 6 months and 1 year respectively but also presents you the opportunity to be a land owners where they so desire.

BritInvest operates on some of our most valuable estates, upon subscription to any of the estates of your choice, investors are offered a plot or plots of land as applicable – a collateral on investment. The product is divided into four categories as described below, designed to enable investors with varying budgets benefit optimally.

Invest Now

At the stage of subscription, the investor will be given 3 options to explore at the end of the 6 and 12 months period:

  • Cash out,
  • Roll over the investment (reinvest) or
  • Take possession of the land or lands as the case may be.
Where the investor opts to cash out, a postdated-cheque containing the total expected amount (capital and interest) will be issued to the investor at the subscription stage. Our aim at BritInvest is to create an enabling platform for our clients (investors) attain set financial goals and financial freedom with the best known methods of investment.
